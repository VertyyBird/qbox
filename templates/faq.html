Frequently Asked Questions

How do questions and anonymity work?

You can submit questions on someone’s profile whether you are logged in or not. Logged-out visitors can only ask anonymously, logged-in users can toggle anonymity before submitting.

Where do I find new questions?

Your Dashboard shows unanswered questions sent to you. The header also shows a count of pending questions. Answering from the dashboard immediately publishes your response to your profile and the public feed.

Other users need to submit questions to you. Qbox does not send automated questions to your dashboard.

Can I make my answers private?

No. All answers are accessible by the public at all times. If you do not wish for your answer or question to be seen by everyone, do not submit that to the site.

How do answer permalinks work?

Each answer gets a shareable link at /user/<username>/a/<id> using a non-sequential public ID. Anyone with the link can read that single answer, even if they aren’t logged in.

How do avatar URLs work?

Avatar URLs must meet the following requirements:

  • Host must be on the allowlist: ibb.co, i.ibb.co, github.com, raw.githubusercontent.com, avatars.githubusercontent.com, user-images.githubusercontent.com.
  • File extension must end with .png, .jpg, .jpeg, .gif, or .webp.
  • The URL must be accessible and respond with a successful status (we check with a quick HEAD/GET request and follow a single redirect).
  • Images must be no larger than 300x300px (3x the rendered size) so pages stay fast.

If your link fails validation, double-check the host and that it points directly to an image file. The best way to be successful is to right click on the image you want to use and then click on "copy image URL"
